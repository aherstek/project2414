Safe School Floor Plan Dashboard

A solution centered on navigating between TCDSB schools and their associated floors, toggling on and off layers to obtain safety layers, etc.

Software used: AutoCAD 3D Map, ArcGIS Pro, ArcGIS Web Maps, ArcGIS Instant Apps Atlas

Features to explore

  • Cover page explaining the Dashboard
  • Introduction page outlining instructions on how to use.
  • Click on a map's point features to see it's associated data.
  • Ability to open different maps, and toggle on and off map layers.
  • Utilize widgets such as 'Adjust Transparency' to view multiple floors at once.

Building 3D Render

A theoretical 3D render of a school.

Software used: ArcGIS Pro, ArcScene, ArcGIS Instant App 3D Viewer

Features to Explore

  • View a 3D visual of school floorplans
  • Turn on/off different layers.
  • Use 'Open Overview Map' widget to bring you to specific views.
  • Click on rooms to learn about it's associated data.

This scene is generated from a Utah Business Building Floor Plan to protect the privacy of the TCDSB.

Sample School Maps

Cartographic Outputs

Exploring ways to display floor plans and it's associated info in a static, 2D format

Software used: ArcGIS Pro, ArcScene

Features to Explore

  • Maps 1 & 2: 3D static maps of a sample school's 1st and 2nd floor use simplied room types and safety exits, allowing staff to understand floor plan layouts efficiently.
  • Maps 3 & 4: Minimalistic polyline outlines allow for safety layers to be the focal point of these 2D maps, with an inset map to allow the user to orient themselves within the school's site.
  • Map 5: Utilizing a muted colour pallete for supplemental layers allow for school locations, in the TCDSB's signature deep red, to stand out within the reference map.
